It's not just Coffee
It's StarBucks

Starbucks is an American multinational company with the largest northern chain of coffee shops in the world. It has its headquarters in the city of Seattle, Washington state. The company created its name partly inspired by the character Starbuck from the book Moby Dick, and its logo is a 16th-century Scandinavian carving of a mermaid with two tails.
